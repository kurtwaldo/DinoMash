Description

During a monster truck rally, a time warp occurred landing you back into Jurassic times. With no fuel, you must utilize the landscape to maintain speed. Mechanically similar to Tiny Wings and Time Surfer, you push a single button to dive down hills, and release it to get air. Mash down on dinosaurs for a bounce, and keep moving with hopes of getting back to the future! There is much to be discovered in this family friendly endless runner!

Features

  • Travel through five spectacular prehistoric enviornments
  • Collect various Trucks, all offering a unique feel to the game! A truck for every personality.
  • Goregous painterly artwork by Hailey McLaughlin with over 20 hand drawn dinosaurs.
  • Completely free to play with no imposing ads
  • Many more suprises! Play to find out (:
