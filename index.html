MORE INFO



Welcome to DinoMash - where time is everything and time is nothing; where creativity and physics are your only way to survive; where you find yourself in stunning prehistoric lands, zooming past hoards of raptors, T-Rexes, and other strange dinosaurs. Welcome to our time warped world where you just have to Keep Calm and RAWR!

DinoMash is an addictive one button driving game that is deceptively simple. Just tap and hold to dive down hills, and release to launch through the air. Surf the bumpy landscape with the ultimate goal of getting as far out as possible, beating your previous distance score, and of course, smashing the leaderboards to bits! Oh and to make things interesting, you are warped back in time and dinosaurs are everywhere! Use them to ‘bounce and boost’ your mad driving skills. Reach crazy fast speeds, soar through the air, and chain bounce to become a DinoMashter!

DinoMash was designed with you in mind. Whether you just have a couple of minutes waiting in line or you have hours to spend lazing by the pool, we’ve got you covered from quick play sessions to ultra competitive, leaderboard-infused long play sessions! All you have to do is take the plunge and hit install to give us a go!

FEATURES

• Simple single tap controls to navigate through challenging obstacles and environments
• Collect gold by driving and completing challenges. From time to time we give you gold - just because you’re you!
• Spectacularly scenic, hand-painted prehistoric environments, vehicles, and creatures
• 30 different vehicles to collect, each offering a unique gameplay experience
• Earn rewards for completing fun round based challenges
• Four separate endless Zones
• Game Center supported Leaderboard system to compete with your friends and show them how it's REALLY done!
• Completely free to play with NO imposing ads. YOU control when you see ads and get rewarded shiny gold nuggets for watching!
• You can unlock all vehicles and zones just by playing, without spending a dime!
